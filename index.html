Cache Karate

What is Shin-Toshi?

Cache Karate is a Shin-Toshi school of martial arts dedicated to cultivating character, control, & confidence in a safe and positive environment for children, teens & adults.


The goal of Cache Karate is to build championship girls and boys and in the process create championship martial artists. There is more to martial arts training than learning to kick, punch and compete-although these skills are essential!


Part of becoming a Martial Artist is to find out who you are inside and build character. At Cache Karate we also teach students to have respect for other people, to set and reach goals and to do all things with integrity.

Our Facilities

Do you want to Teach Arobics, Dance or Cheer?

Cache Karate has an awesome facility and is looking for partners to share space at the new Cache Karate Dojo in Richmond! Highlights of the school include:

  • 30x40 2" foam cheer-grade flooring
  • 25 feet of full length mirrors
  • ADA approved restroom & drinking fountain
  • changing rooms
  • cubbies & hooks for student equipment
  • parent viewing area
  • equipment storage
  • conference room available
  • large gravel parking area & grassy outdoor space and a great location on Highway 91 in Richmond
If you have talents and skills and need a professional space to host your classes, please contact cachekarate@gmail.com for details and to set up an appointment!
