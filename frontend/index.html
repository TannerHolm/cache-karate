What is Shin-Toshi?

Cache Karate is a Shin-Toshi school of martial arts dedicated to cultivating character, control, & confidence in a safe and positive environment for children, teens & adults.


The goal of Cache Karate is to build championship girls and boys and in the process create championship martial artists. There is more to martial arts training than learning to kick, punch and compete-although these skills are essential!


Part of becoming a Martial Artist is to find out who you are inside and build character. At Cache Karate we also teach students to have respect for other people, to set and reach goals and to do all things with integrity.

Nano Ninjas

Cache Karate's Ninja program is designed for the youngest martial artist, childred aged 4-7 years old. This once/week 45 minute class centers on respect and self-discipline, listening and following instructions, self-defense and and safety skills and having fun learning to control their small bodies at karate. Cache Karate Ninjas follow a special age-appropriate Ninja Stripes program designed by Sensei judd that corresponds with the belt requirements for the early belt rank advancement curriculum of Karate Shin-Toshi. This program allows the Little Ninjas opportunities to earn small acheivements and promotions while working toward their big goal of black belt. Classes are taught by Mr. Tanner Holm.


Nano Ninjas is geared toward towards 4-5 year old preschoolers and more emphasis is placed on listening and following instructions, and learning body control. Nano Ninja students flow into the Little Ninjas class when they have entered kindergarten or when they have demonstrated the skills for the next level!

Regular Class

This twice/week class is for students aged 7-13 years old. Students will learn basic self-defense techniques and elements of martial arts as they attain the first ranks of Karate Shin-Toshi. Focus and respect is taught and expected in every class and is backed up with positive feedback and encouragement. Students will also learn the importance of warming-up, stretching appropriately and using control at all times. There are currently two Beginning Karate Kids class offerings at Cache-Karate--all new Karate Kid students begin their training in one of these Beginning I classes.

Our Instructors

Sensei Leslie Judd

Head instructor, Owner

Sensei Leslie Judd is the owner and Head Instructor at Cache Karate. She earned her 1st Dan Black Belt as a teenager and taught karate students of all ages and ranks through her teenage years. She successfully tested for her 2nd Dan Black Belt in April 2012, weeks after opening Cache Karate in Richmond, Utah.
In addition to her martial arts accomplishments, Ms. Judd also holds a B.A. in International Studies from USU and has spent time in China teaching English to Chinese children. While there, she briefly studied Kung Fu and Tai Chi, broadening her cultural and martial arts exposure. Ms. Judd is also a wife and mother to three future Black Belt boys!
Opening a Dojo is the realization of a dream Ms. Judd has pursued since she was 13 years old. She takes great satisfaction in her students' personal growth as they become martial artists. Ms. Judd is an advocate for education in all forms and is thrilled to be teaching Karate in Cache Karate.

Mr. Tanner Holm

Little Ninjas Instructor

Tanner Holm is the Little Ninjas instructor, ranking as purple belt in Shin-Toshi Karate. Having a deep interest in martial arts, Tanner started Shin-Toshi as a teenager and resumed his training in Shin-Toshi eight months ago. In between his training as a teenager and his recent return to Shin-Toshi, Tanner participated in martial arts such as Brazilian Jujitsu and other styles associated with MMA (Mixed Martial Arts) fighting style. This exposure has helped Tanner appreciate the beauty behind the artistic forms of the Shin-Toshi style while emphasizing it's advantage in direct and powerful self-defense.
Tanner is a husband and proud father of his own soon-to-be little ninja son, and is currently attending Utah State University.
